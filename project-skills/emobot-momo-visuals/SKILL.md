## Workflow

1. Decide whether the task is better as code-native UI or bitmap art.
2. Keep momo cute before clever.
3. Use softness, light, translucency, and scale to show change.
4. Avoid aggressive contrast, hard edges, armor, weapons, or game-like cosmetics.
5. Version outputs cleanly and move approved assets into the workspace.
