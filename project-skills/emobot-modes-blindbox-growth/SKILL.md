## Workflow

1. Confirm whether the change belongs to Mood Weather, a mode scene, Blind Box Lite, or growth.
2. Keep the three mode scenes as micro-interactions, not games.
3. Keep rewards light and deterministic.
4. Never expand into levels, rankings, item economy, or large collection systems.
5. Keep names aligned across frontend, backend, analytics, and QA.
